Project Overview:

This dashboard presents valuable information about the bacterial species found in the belly button's of the participants in the research's sample survey. The results of the sample's test have been displayed in the form of visualizations. The participants and researchers can visit this page, select an id and see the results presenting details about bacteria living in the bellies of participants.
